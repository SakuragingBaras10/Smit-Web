About Senior High School

The Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. Senior High School is setting the bar in the delivery of outcomes-based education in Mindanao and the rest of the world. With its blended-learning approach, highly-qualified faculty members, learner-centered curricula, and Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. Senior High School provides its students with global skills, competencies, and knowledge that will lead to successful career paths in the future.

The Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. High School opened its doors to its pioneering students last July 2018. Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. SHS department offers both the Academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Tracks.
