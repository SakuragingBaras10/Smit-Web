Who We Are
The Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. Provides Basic And Advance Learning To Students. We are a Non-Sectarian Institution With An Effective And Effecient Workforce Who Comits To Deliver A' Innovative And Responsive Teaching Strategies And Will Produce An Ethical Research Oriented Graduates

Vision

Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. is a learner centered Institution commited to quality education.

Mission

Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc. is a Non-sectarian institution that Provides basic and advance learning to students with Quality and holistic Education.

  • effecient and effective faculty and staff
  • adequate and appropriate instructional facilities
  • Innovative and reponsive teaching strategies

Core Values
