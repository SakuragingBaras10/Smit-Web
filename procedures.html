Enrollment Procedures

Step 1 SECURING FORMS

Incoming Freshmen

  • Proceed to the Program Head where you intend to enroll and ask for pre-enrollment form.
  • Proceed to Cashier's Office for the payment of your registration fee.

Transferes and Old Students

  • Proceed to Program Head for Evaluation.
  • Secure subject loads and pre-enrollment form from your Program Head.
  • Proceed to the Cashier's Office for the enrollment fee.

Step 2 FILLING UP THE OFFICIAL ENROLLMENT FORM

  • Fill up the official enrollment form based on the subject loads on your pre-enrollment form.
  • Have your class schedules checked and controlled by the program head.
  • Proceed to the Office of the NSTP Coordinator at SMIT Assessment Center(LTS and CWTS) for enrollment and for ROTC at Student's Center in front of the Main Building

Step 3 FINAL PROCESSING

  • Proceed to the Registrar's Office
  • Submit Requirements for final processing
  • Have your subject load stamped SMIT ENROLLED
  • Congratulations You are now officially enrolled at SMIT
